Projects

Windmill Prediction Project

August 2021 - December 2021

In my fall semester of Freshman Year I took Energy Analytics (a Freshman Research Initiative Class). Where we were taught the basics of how to use python within the scope of a Jupyter Notebook to create a reserach project. My team choose to conduct research on the relation between Windmills in the continental United States and the Windspeed within those states. Furthermore our reserach highlighted certain states in which Windmills were not as prevalent despite appealing windspeeds. However we only moderately covered external factors outside of windspeed such as terrain, environmental factors, other prexsisting renewal energy sources, etc.

PintOS

August 2023 - December 2023

Project that was completed throughout the semester for Operating Systems class at UT Austin. The PintOS Systems allowed me to implement a very bare bones opertaing system for the x86 architecture. I wrote the code for the missing componenets using C and learned how memory management, file management, threads, and user programs work. Allowing me to gain a understanding of the innerworkings of these systems and apply them in a practical bare-bones setting.

Occupations and Mental Health

January 2024 - May 2024

Throughout this semester I implemented three different machine learning algorithms that consisted of Decision Trees, Apriori, and a Neural Net. These were used to find relations between varrying job conditions and how they impact workers' mental health. One of the main factors found to have an effect on mental health was presence of family history of mental issues.

Collection Mobile Application

January 2024 - May 2024

Using react, my team built a mobile application built around allowing users to keep track of their personal media collections (video games, movies, music). We used prexsisting databases of data from IMDB, Steam, etc. to populate entries within user's collections and kept track of users using a firebase database. The front-end design of the website was coded with the help of bootstrap to help our design look sleek and intuitive.
